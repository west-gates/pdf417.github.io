About

I am pursuing a PhD in computer science with focus on machine learning and data mining, under the supervision from Dr. Sanjay Ranka. My research interests lie in digging out unrevealed knowledge from various data sources by designing and applying new machine learning methods. Currently I am working with heterogeneous medical datasets to develop data-driven personalized medicine applications, leveraging machine learning (especially deep learning) and computer vision techniques.
